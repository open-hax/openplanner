**Knoxx** is a multi-tenant knowledge platform that turns your clients’ documentation, wikis, tickets, and internal know-how into a searchable, AI-assisted knowledge base.

### What Knoxx Delivers

| Capability | Client Value |
|---------|---------|
| **Automated ingestion** | Pulls in docs, wikis, and support tickets with minimal manual effort |
| **Semantic search** | Finds answers by meaning, not just exact keywords |
| **Multilingual translation** | Makes technical content usable across regions and teams |
| **Expert review workflow** | Lets subject-matter experts review, correct, and approve outputs |
| **Continuous improvement loop** | Uses approved corrections to improve answer quality over time |
| **Layered access model** | Supports public assistants, CMS workflows, internal knowledge, and developer tooling |

### Why It Fits FutureSight

FutureSight’s clients—especially in manufacturing, defence, and complex technical sales—deal with large product catalogs, fragmented documentation, multilingual content, compliance pressure, and long sales cycles.

Knoxx gives those clients a knowledge system that improves with expert input, while giving FutureSight a repeatable service offering with ongoing revenue instead of one-off project work.

## Commercial Model

### Open Source + Proprietary

| Open Source Foundation | Proprietary Service Layer |
|-------------|-------------|
| OpenPlanner data layer | Client-specific integrations |
| Review and labeling components | Deployment, operations, and monitoring |
| Chat UI building blocks | Tenant onboarding and training workflows |
| Ingestion utilities | Delivery methodology and system fit |

**Positioning:** clients get transparency, auditability, and reduced vendor lock-in; FutureSight keeps the high-value integration, deployment, and operational layer.
